## Research Summary
My research focuses on the intersection of **Precision Manufacturing**, **Surgical Robotics**, and **Biomedical Engineering**. I specialize in the fundamental mechanics of **bone-tool interaction**, focusing on thermal modeling and force analysis to improve the safety and accuracy of orthopedic procedures. My recent work extends into **Soft Tissue Biomechanics** and **Intelligent Medical Devices**, including the development and review of **magnetically actuated capsule robots** for targeted drug delivery and in-situ gastrointestinal visualization. By bridging advanced manufacturing techniques with clinical needs, I aim to develop the next generation of minimally invasive surgical tools and robotic systems.
